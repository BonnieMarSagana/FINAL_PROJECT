My Journey

Hello, I'm Bonnie Mar Sagana, and my journey into the world of YouTube began during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic. Faced with the need for solace and connection, I discovered a profound source of comfort in playing video games with friends. Motivated by the inspiration drawn from other content creators on the platform, I decided to embark on my own YouTube adventure.

Determined to share my gaming experiences and create a community where others could find joy and camaraderie, I delved into the realm of video editing, teaching myself the intricacies of the craft. Explore my video portfolio as I showcase my passion for gaming and the storytelling artistry I've cultivated through my journey into content creation.
