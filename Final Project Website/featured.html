STORYBOARD TEST

This video was filmed prior to the short film. The purpose of this test is to explore ideas on how to execute and film this story (Written by Leandro Dela Cruz). Additionally, we tested the rough ideas from the rough storyboard to see if they would look good on camera.

The location of this Video Test was at Pau Pau Beach with Leandro Dela Cruz as the Main Character and Jody Coloma as the victim in the car. The equipment used to film was a digital camera and a gimbal to capture high-quality and stabilized footage.

4-H AMONG US TOURNAMENT

The 4-H Among Us Tournament was filmed and edited during the COVID-19 pandemic. To stay engaged with other 4-Hers, we organized an online tournament with the popular video game, "Among Us". This online tournament provided easy access to other 4-Hers on the island amidst the pandemic. In this tournament, each participant works and strives for the same goal.
